Who we are

We prefer to run things like a hair salon. We schedule your appointment for when we are going to groom your dog. No need to sit in a cage all day. Though we are flexible with scheduling, we try to keep as few dogs in the salon as possible. This eliminates excess noise and stress for everyone. We will give you a call or text as we are finishing up so you can come fetch your dog.
